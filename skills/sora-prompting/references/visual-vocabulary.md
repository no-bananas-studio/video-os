# Sora Visual Vocabulary Reference

Precise terminology for describing visual elements in Sora prompts.

## Material Descriptions

### Fabric & Texture
- Crushed velvet
- Crisp linen
- Worn leather
- Brushed cotton
- Silk sheen
- Rough burlap
- Knit wool
- Distressed denim

### Surface Quality
- Polished to a mirror finish
- Matte and non-reflective
- Weathered and patinated
- Freshly painted
- Dusty and neglected
- Rain-slicked
- Frost-covered
- Sun-bleached

### Metal Types
- Brushed stainless steel
- Aged brass with verdigris
- Chrome with fingerprints
- Rusted iron
- Polished copper
- Matte black anodized
- Tarnished silver

## Light Quality Descriptors

### Source Quality
- Hard light (sharp shadows)
- Soft light (diffused shadows)
- Dappled light (broken by objects)
- Bounced light (reflected from surface)
- Practical light (from visible source in scene)
- Motivated light (justified by scene element)

### Direction Terms
- Key light from upper left
- Fill from camera position
- Rim light from behind
- Underlit from below
- Side-lit, half face in shadow
- Top-lit, eyes in shadow

### Color Temperature
- Tungsten warm (2700K orange)
- Daylight balanced (5600K neutral)
- Overcast cool (6500K blue)
- Candlelight (1800K deep amber)
- Fluorescent green cast
- LED neutral white

### Building Elements
- Crown molding with egg-and-dart pattern
- Exposed brick, painted white
- Industrial steel beams, riveted
- Art deco geometric inlays
- Mid-century modern clean lines
- Victorian ornate woodwork

### Windows & Light
- Floor-to-ceiling warehouse windows
- Small diamond-paned casement
- Frosted glass diffusing light
- Venetian blinds casting stripes
- Sheer curtains glowing with backlight

### Floors & Walls
- Herringbone hardwood, honey-toned
- Cracked terrazzo, institutional
- Rough concrete, industrial
- Worn marble, veined gray
- Peeling wallpaper, faded floral

## Human Details

### Skin & Complexion
- Warm undertones, sun-kissed
- Cool undertones, porcelain
- Weathered and lined
- Youthful, dewy
- Freckled across nose and cheeks
- Flushed from exertion

### Hair Descriptions
- Silver-gray, swept back
- Dark curls, unruly
- Blonde, poker-straight
- Auburn waves, shoulder-length
- Buzz-cut, salt and pepper
- Windswept, strands across face

### Eye Characteristics
- Deep-set, shadowed
- Wide-set, alert
- Hooded, weary
- Bright, curious
- Narrowed, suspicious
- Glistening with tears

### Expression Specifics
- Micro-expression of doubt
- Slight downturn at mouth corners
- One eyebrow raised
- Jaw clenched, temples tight
- Eyes crinkling with genuine smile
- Nostrils flaring with anger

## Environmental Details

### Weather Specifics
- Light drizzle, not quite rain
- Heavy fog, visibility under 50 feet
- Gentle snowfall, large flakes
- Heat shimmer rising from asphalt
- Dust devils spinning
- Storm clouds building on horizon

### Time Markers
- Blue hour, just after sunset
- Magic hour, golden side-light
- High noon, harsh overhead
- 3 AM, empty streets
- First light, pre-sunrise glow
- Approaching twilight

### Atmosphere
- Haze softening distance
- Crystal clear, sharp to horizon
- Smoke hanging in air
- Steam rising from vents
- Dust motes in light beams
- Pollen floating

## Motion Descriptions

### Speed Terms
- Glacially slow
- Deliberate and measured
- Brisk, purposeful
- Hurried, almost running
- Sudden, startling
- Languid, unhurried

### Quality of Movement
- Fluid and graceful
- Jerky and uncertain
- Mechanical, robotic
- Natural, unstudied
- Exaggerated, theatrical
- Restrained, controlled

### Gesture Specifics
- Hand brushes hair from face
- Fingers drum on surface
- Shoulders rise and drop in sigh
- Chin lifts defiantly
- Eyes dart to the left
- Throat swallows nervously

## Combining Vocabulary

Layer specific terms for rich descriptions:

```
A weathered leather armchair, cracked and faded to cognac brown,
sits in a pool of dusty afternoon light from tall warehouse windows.
Dust motes drift lazily in the golden beams.
```

```
Her silver-gray hair, swept back from a face weathered by decades
of coastal wind, catches the last orange light of sunset. Deep lines
bracket a slight, knowing smile.
```

```
Fingers drum once on the polished mahogany desk, stop. The hand
flattens, presses down. A deliberate breath. The hand lifts, moves
to the telephone.
```
